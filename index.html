How Community Gallery Works

A complete platform to organize and share your photos with ease

1. Create Account

Sign up with a username and password — your gallery is private and secure.

2. Upload Photos

Drag & drop or click to upload. Add event names to organize by category.

3. Smart Organization

Filter by event, search by title — keep everything tidy and findable.

4. Engage & Share

Like photos, add comments, and build your community around memories.

✨ Key Features

  • Secure user authentication (signup / login)
  • Photo upload with event categorization
  • Search and filter by event or title
  • Like and comment on photos
  • Dark mode support
  • All photos saved in your browser (localStorage) — no server needed
  • Fully responsive design — works on phone, tablet, desktop

📸 Your photos stay private to your account. Each user has their own separate gallery.
